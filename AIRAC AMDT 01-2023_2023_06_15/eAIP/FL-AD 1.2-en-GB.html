1.2.1 RESCUE AND FIRE FIGHTING SERVICES

At aerodromes approved for scheduled and/or non-scheduled traffic with aeroplanes carrying passengers, rescue and fire fighting services are established in accordance with the regulations for Civil Aviation.
Information about whether there is service and what the extent of that service is, is given on page AD 2.2 for each aerodrome.
Scheduled or non-scheduled commercial flights are not normally allowed to use aerodromes without rescue and fire fighting services.
