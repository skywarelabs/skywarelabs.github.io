FLCP AD 2.20 LOCAL AERODROME REGULATIONS
All flights within Lusaka FIR at or below FL150 within and outside controlled airspace shall be operated in accordance with instrument/visual flight rules. Flights above FL150 within and outside controlled airspace shall be operated in accordance with instrument flight rules only.
