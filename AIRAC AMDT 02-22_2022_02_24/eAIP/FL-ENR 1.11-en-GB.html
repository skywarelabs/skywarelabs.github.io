ENR 1.11 ADDRESSING OF FLIGHT PLAN MESSAGES

 
Flight movement messages relating to traffic into or via the Lusaka FIR shall be addressed as stated below in order to warrant correct relay and delivery.
All flights into or via Lusaka FIR shall be addressed to FLKKZPZX and FLFIZQZX

NOTE : Flight movement messages in this context comprise flight plan messages, amendment messages relating thereto and flight plan cancellation messages (ICAO PANS-ATM, Doc 4444, Part VIII, 2.1.1.3 refers).
